Why now?

COVID-19 has locked-down millions of people all over the world in their homes with limited access to medical care and well-being resources. Not so fun, to say the least, but the silver lining is a fabulous opportunity to push the “restart button” and live a happier healthier life by easily utilizing a wealth of health and wellness resources that are available to all of us immediately and cost-effectively. But where do we start? That’s where FestiHealth comes in!

We’re here to open a door for everyone to a better future and a new exciting world of innovative virtual and digital health and well-being solutions that make health and care much better, more customized, and more accessible. Show more...

We will do so by fostering vibrant conversations about this brave new world. From opportunities vs. challenges, to cutting edge technology vs. tradition and best practices we will help clarify the road to a sustainably healthy future for all. We will not shy away from discussing the virtues of changing existing norms, but at the same time we will not forget the importance of safety, security, and privacy for all.

We will share inspiring stories not only about innovation and efficacy of new effective and easy to use technology, but also about resilience and about investing in one’s mind, body, spirit, and soul. We will also facilitate connections between new affordable solutions, free solutions, and users in need to foster a new era where unemployment and physical distance can be alleviated by more efficient and effective virtual health solutions available to all.

And we’re all in this together! That’s why we’re excited to bring together innovators, experts, doctors, nurses, artists, medical professionals, caregivers, patients, parents, students, friends, athletes, policymakers, scientists, philanthropists, hipsters, leaders, and more to Learn, Experience and Connect in FestiHealth 2020.
